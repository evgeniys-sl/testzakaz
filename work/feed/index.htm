Description

Sportening is an iOS and Android app for every sport fan out there. With its strong fan centric approach, it enables fans to focus on teams and competitions they care about.

You asked and you got it.
Available on both biggest platforms.

Sportening is designed for both iOS and Android platforms, meaning that every fan gets a seat at the table.

Strong branding and visual identity speak for themselves.

Sportening is for everyone. Sportening is fresh and cool, but also friendly. Bold titles, bright colors combined with easy to read text and dark overlays.

Get instant notifications and support from Pepper.

Create chat groups, discuss with friends, get news, comment on match details, and watch exclusive goal videos.

A big number of teams and leagues was not a problem once we built a system for generating headers .

Go through every match event on the timeline. For this feature, we worked on dozens of miniature icons for every possible match scenario.
