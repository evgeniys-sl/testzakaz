Description

Altima is an IoT — city management platform that is used to manage assets like lighting, parking and other communal resources.

The lighting module handles lighting profile scheduling, sensor maintenance, lamp grouping and many other administrative features.

We also worked on the parking dashboard module as well as a citizen facing app for parking availability and payment. The dashboard consisted of capacity, sensor and maintenance management features.
